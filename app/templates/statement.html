STATEMENT OF NEED

TCSC, has done tremendous work with community of Mt Elgon over the past 10 years since 2009.

This kind of healing work was started on the mountain shortly after election violence took grip in an already violent community.

After several trainings of the Ex-Militia Members, it has given us a new page, a new leaf and it has been like a new dawn in the peace work.

 

After these past 10 years, we are now realizing that the needs are so big.

As healing continues to take root in souls and mind of the Beneficiary of Mt Elgon, they realize just how deep and complex trauma is in shaping a community and human.

A lot of voices have spoken and testify how the program has moved them and they have a big shift in their social lifestyle and their livelihood.

Still more young people who have not undergone the trainings are still in need of these trainings.

Our work revolves around healing and creating a positive Respond to the social challenges we are facing.

Now we are faced by the need of a complex community that is rooted in deep cultural patterns that again hurts children and

women without pay attention to most of the things surrounding peaceful healing. Some of the key things that we realized are listed below;

a.      Women empowerment, in all the dimension women are subject and not equals to me.

This being a big challenge in the whole world, issues like using family planning are not talked about and thus one can have as many children as they believe God gives them.

So many parents are very young and get themselves having children that it becomes very difficult to support their medical, educational and basic needs.

One of the basic program is empowering women and help each other to look at how to bring in life a health family and learning to talk about feelings with their men.

 

b.      Both the girls and young children are facing so many risks in life. Girls are sexually abused by either relative, teachers or motorbike transporters just in the name that they cannot afford Sanitary Towels thus they are at the mercies of this individual at a certain time of their life. In just 7 months defilement of minors’ case became so rampant and it brought a lot of concerns.

The sad thing is when a parent who is supposed to protect the child especially the fathers go ahead and negotiate payments just to get rid of the Law despite whatever laws are in the country. In this the woman has no say and thus the victims usually end up not having justice. As we talked to the police hoping to get help in stopping this, we only realized even women when they go to fetch firewood from the forest, they are raped but can’t say to the administration because they feel it is a shame.

 

c.      The community still deeply use the skills of tradition midwives, who again are not having any experience.

A lot of mortality death is experienced but not reported since they try to help but have a difficult time if it is a complicated birth. Again, it is a culture challenge where most women will not go to hospital because they don’t want the male doctors to attend to them, issues related to HIV is still needs a lot of awareness.

There is little knowledge about STI and HIV.

 

d.      There are lots of Orphans that have been neglected in the care of elderly grandparents or abusive relative,

they manage to go to school on a good day but without school uniforms they feel odd and unsettled to be in class,

so some opt go for manual jobs and save money to buy books and school uniforms. Girls stay out of school during

their menstrual circles and even as they go to school they have never experience people who visit and give motivational talk.

In some situations, teachers get overwhelmed because of the number of students they serve and lessons per day that they lose to concentrate on follow up.

 

e.      The Organization was founded with the community need of having a peace center,

to play as a neutral place where the communities from both clans can meet and share skills and have dialogues about the progress of their communities.

We have a two and half acre of land and In it there is a small building that can’t fit the number of participants in some of the meetings at the same time have

facilitators stay in especially when it rains. This center is constructed it will give some of the orphan who are having extreme harsh condition a home to feel love and

acceptance and look at life with hope, it will also provide a library for school kids and high school students to have time of reading and opening their mind wider with that opportunity.

Plan is also to have a workshop conference with rooms that communities can use for peaceful and economic trainings as they happen.
