About

TCSC began with a group of devoted peacemakers working together to strengthen communities through Alternative to Violence Project (AVP), and Healing and Rebuilding Our Communities (HROC) workshops. Quaker organizations (African Great Lakes initiative, Friends United Meeting and the American Friends Service Committee) have supported the training of facilitators of AVP and HROC in much of East Africa.

MISSION/ VISION

To promote a culture of peace and restoration of justice through Advocacy, economic empowerment and community development.
